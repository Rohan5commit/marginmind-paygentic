# Project Overview

## Product Title

MarginMind

## Concise Problem Summary

Founders and small teams regularly overspend on SaaS because duplicate tools, rising vendor costs, low-usage subscriptions, and refund-worthy renewals hide inside normal operating spend.

## Concise Solution Summary

MarginMind ingests expense data, finds waste, explains the highest-value savings opportunities, and prepares Locus-native actions the agent can take to reduce spend.

## What The Product Does

- loads sample startup spend or uploaded CSV data
- detects recurring subscriptions and vendor patterns
- identifies duplicate tools, underused software, pricing outliers, rising bills, refund windows, and negotiation candidates
- estimates monthly and annual savings
- proposes wallet-funded Locus actions and ROI-aware task escalations
- exports a founder-ready savings report

## How It Makes Or Saves Money

MarginMind saves money by surfacing avoidable spend and converting it into concrete action:
- cancel redundant subscriptions
- downgrade underused plans
- recover recent charges through refund requests
- negotiate annual contracts where savings justify the effort
- deploy recurring monitoring so waste does not return
